# NNNN. Short noun phrase describing the decision

Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Status: Proposed

## Context

What is the forcing function? What constraints, pressures, or facts make a decision necessary right now? Describe the situation objectively. A reader who has never seen this codebase should be able to tell why the decision could not be deferred.

Avoid the word "we" deciding things in the Context — that belongs in the Decision. Avoid writing in the voice of an advocate here; the Context is where honest tradeoffs live.

## Decision

State the decision in one or two sentences. Use active voice: "We will use X." Then, if useful, expand on the shape of the decision in a short paragraph. Name the alternatives that were considered and why they lost.

## Consequences

Describe what becomes true after this decision is in effect — positive, negative, and neutral. Include:

- What now becomes easier.
- What now becomes harder, or costs more.
- What future work this unlocks or forecloses.
- Any new obligations (runbooks to write, skills to keep on the team, vendors to monitor).

Honesty here is the whole point. An ADR that lists only upsides is a press release, not a record.
